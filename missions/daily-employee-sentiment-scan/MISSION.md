# Daily Employee Sentiment Scan

Analyze team communication tone and flag potential morale issues early.

## Prompt

Use #Employee engagement to scan team communication channels (Slack, email, project tools) for sentiment patterns over the past 24 hours. Apply #Onboarding docs to cross-reference new hire communication patterns against healthy benchmarks. Use #Resume screening to identify any team members who may be updating external profiles (potential flight risk signals). Use #Candidate outreach to assess internal mobility interest signals. Compile a daily sentiment report with team-level mood scores, flagged individuals needing check-ins, and recommended HR actions.
