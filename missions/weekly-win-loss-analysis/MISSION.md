# Weekly Win/Loss Analysis

Analyze closed deals, extract winning patterns, and update the sales playbook.

## Prompt

Use #CRM management to pull all deals closed this week, both won and lost. Use #Pipeline reporting to analyze deal velocity, average deal size, and conversion rates by stage. Apply #Prospect research to identify common traits among won deals (industry, company size, pain points) and lost deals (objections, competitors, timing). Use #Lead qualification to refine scoring criteria based on patterns. Compile a win/loss report with updated playbook recommendations and share with the team.
