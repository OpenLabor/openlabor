# Monthly Competitor Brand Audit

Analyze competitor visual identities and identify differentiation opportunities.

## Prompt

Use #Logo concepts to collect and analyze the visual identities of our top 5 competitors including logos, color palettes, and typography choices. Apply #Color palette selection to map the competitive color landscape and identify underused color territories. Use #Style guide creation to compare brand personality attributes across competitors. Use #Brand assets to assess how our visual identity stands out or blends in. Compile a competitive brand audit with visual comparisons, differentiation gaps, and strategic recommendations for strengthening our visual position.
