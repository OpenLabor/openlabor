# Monthly Brand Consistency Report

Audit all customer-facing assets for brand alignment and visual consistency.

## Prompt

Use #Design system management to catalog all customer-facing assets across web, email, social, and print. Use #Brand assets to compare each asset against current brand guidelines for color, typography, imagery style, and tone. Apply #Image editing to create side-by-side comparisons of inconsistent assets versus the approved standard. Use #Social media graphics to check that social templates are current. Compile a brand consistency scorecard with grades by channel, specific violations, and a prioritized remediation plan.
