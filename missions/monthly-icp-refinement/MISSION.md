# Monthly ICP Refinement

Analyze won and lost deals to refine the ideal customer profile and improve targeting.

## Prompt

Use #Pipeline reporting to pull all deals from the past month with outcomes and metadata. Use #ICP targeting to compare current ICP criteria against actual closed-won characteristics. Apply #Lead qualification to analyze which scoring factors best predicted success. Use #Contact enrichment to identify new firmographic or technographic attributes common among best customers. Update the ICP document with revised criteria, scoring weights, and exclusion rules. Output a summary of changes with supporting data.
