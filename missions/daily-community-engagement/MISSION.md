# Daily Community Engagement

Respond to comments and DMs, and foster community discussions across all social platforms.

## Prompt

Use #Community building to scan all social platforms for new comments, DMs, and community discussions from the past 24 hours. Use #Engagement management to prioritize responses by urgency and opportunity value. Apply #Tone-of-voice adaptation to ensure all responses match brand personality. Use #Trend monitoring to identify trending conversations where the brand can add value. Draft thoughtful replies for high-priority interactions and schedule community-building posts to spark discussion.
