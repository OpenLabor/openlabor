# Monthly Brand Health Report

Track brand perception, social mentions, and SEO brand term performance.

## Prompt

Use #Brand naming to audit current brand asset usage across all channels for consistency. Apply #Trademark screening to monitor for any new trademark filings that could conflict with our brand. Use #SEO optimization to track branded search term volume and ranking positions. Compile a brand health scorecard with perception metrics, threat alerts, and strategic recommendations.
