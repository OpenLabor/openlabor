# Weekly Cash Flow Forecast

Project cash position for the next 4 weeks and flag upcoming shortfalls.

## Prompt

Use #Cash flow tracking to pull current cash balances across all accounts and pending transactions. Use #Invoice processing to compile all outstanding receivables and payables with expected dates. Apply #Expense categorization to analyze recurring expenses and seasonal patterns. Use #Financial reporting to project weekly cash positions for the next 4 weeks under best-case, expected, and worst-case scenarios. Flag any weeks where projected cash falls below operating minimums. Output a cash flow forecast with visual timeline, risk flags, and recommended actions to address shortfalls.
