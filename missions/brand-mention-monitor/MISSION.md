# Brand Mention Monitor

Track brand mentions across social platforms and flag engagement opportunities or PR risks.

## Prompt

Use #Trend monitoring to scan X, LinkedIn, and relevant forums for mentions of our brand, products, and key team members. Use #Engagement management to categorize each mention as positive, neutral, or negative. For positive mentions, draft a thank-you reply. For negative mentions, flag for review and draft a professional response. Use #Community building to identify potential brand advocates from recurring positive mentions. Compile a summary report.
