# Daily Engagement Sweep

Reply to mentions, engage with industry posts, and grow your network on X every day.

## Prompt

Use #Engagement management to scan all new mentions, replies, and DMs from the past 24 hours. Use #Community building to identify high-value industry posts to engage with thoughtful replies. Apply #Audience growth tactics to follow and interact with accounts in our target audience. Use #X analytics & reporting to track engagement rate trends and identify which interactions drive the most profile visits. Log all engagement activity for weekly reporting.
