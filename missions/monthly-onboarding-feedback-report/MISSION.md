# Monthly Onboarding Feedback Report

Survey recent hires, analyze feedback, and identify onboarding process gaps.

## Prompt

Use #Onboarding docs to review the current onboarding checklist and materials for completeness. Use #Employee engagement to collect and analyze feedback from all employees who started in the past 60 days. Apply #Candidate outreach to compare pre-hire expectations with post-hire experience. Use #Interview scheduling to assess whether the interview-to-start timeline is meeting targets. Compile a monthly onboarding report with satisfaction scores, common pain points, suggested improvements, and a comparison against previous months.
