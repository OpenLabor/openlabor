# Weekly Lead List Builder

Build a fresh list of ICP-matched leads enriched with contact data every week.

## Prompt

Use #ICP targeting to define this week's search criteria based on our ideal customer profile. Use #Lead scraping to find 100 new prospects matching the criteria across LinkedIn and company databases. Apply #Contact enrichment to append verified emails, phone numbers, company size, and funding stage. Use #Intent signal detection to prioritize leads showing active buying signals. Export the final list in CRM-ready format using #Lead list building.
