# Monthly Financial Close

Reconcile all accounts, generate P&L statement, and prepare balance sheet.

## Prompt

Use #Expense categorization to review and finalize all transaction categorizations for the closing month. Apply #Receipt scanning to match any remaining unreconciled receipts and flag missing documentation. Use #Cash flow tracking to reconcile all bank accounts and credit cards against book balances. Use #Invoice processing to close out all receivable and payable entries for the period. Use #Financial reporting to generate a complete P&L statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Flag any discrepancies exceeding thresholds and output a close checklist with sign-off status.
