# Monthly Market Research Report

Analyze the competitive landscape, market trends, and opportunity sizing.

## Prompt

Use #SQL queries to pull internal market segment data including customer demographics, deal sizes, and win rates by vertical. Apply #Trend analysis to identify growing and shrinking market segments over the past 3 months. Use #Forecasting to estimate total addressable market size for each segment. Use #Anomaly detection to flag any sudden competitive shifts or market disruptions. Compile a comprehensive market research report with competitive landscape overview, trend analysis, opportunity sizing by segment, and strategic recommendations for market expansion.
