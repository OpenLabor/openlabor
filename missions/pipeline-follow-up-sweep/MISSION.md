# Pipeline Follow-Up Sweep

Review stale deals and send follow-ups to re-engage cold prospects in the pipeline.

## Prompt

Use #CRM management to pull all deals that have been inactive for more than 5 days. Use #Prospect research to check for any new developments at the prospect's company. Use #Email personalization to draft re-engagement emails referencing the new intel or offering fresh value. Apply #Lead qualification to flag any deals that should be marked as lost. Update deal stages and notes in the CRM using #Pipeline reporting.
