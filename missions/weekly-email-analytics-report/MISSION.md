# Weekly Email Analytics Report

Track email response rates, average reply time, and flag items needing attention.

## Prompt

Use #Follow-up tracking to compile statistics on all emails sent and received this week. Use #Email triage to calculate average response times for incoming and outgoing emails. Apply #Priority inbox rules to identify any high-priority emails that went unanswered or had delayed responses. Use #Email drafting to assess the quality and consistency of sent responses. Compile a weekly email analytics report with response rates, average reply times, unanswered threads, and recommendations for improving email efficiency.
