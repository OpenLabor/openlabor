# Monthly Process Audit

Review operational workflows, identify bottlenecks, and recommend process improvements.

## Prompt

Use #Workflow optimization to map all active operational processes and measure cycle times against benchmarks. Use #KPI tracking to identify processes with declining efficiency or increasing error rates. Apply #SOPs to review whether current standard operating procedures are being followed and are still relevant. Use #Team coordination to gather feedback from team leads on pain points and workaround patterns. Compile a process audit report with bottleneck analysis, root causes, and a prioritized improvement roadmap with expected impact.
