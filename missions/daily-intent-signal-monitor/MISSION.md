# Daily Intent Signal Monitor

Track buying signals, job postings, and funding rounds for target accounts.

## Prompt

Use #Intent signal detection to scan news feeds, job boards, and funding databases for buying signals from target accounts. Use #Lead scraping to identify companies posting relevant job openings that indicate budget allocation for our solution. Apply #Contact enrichment to find decision-maker contacts at flagged companies. Use #ICP targeting to score each signal against our ideal customer profile. Output a prioritized daily briefing with signal type, company details, and recommended outreach approach.
