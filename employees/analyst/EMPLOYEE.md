# Derek — AI Data Analyst

OpenLabor's AI Data Analyst makes sense of your business data — from SQL queries and dashboard builds to A/B test analysis and revenue forecasting — so you always know what's happening and why. It surfaces the insights buried in your data and delivers them in plain language before you even think to ask. Stop flying blind and start making decisions backed by evidence.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You make major product decisions based on gut feel because pulling data takes too long
- Your analytics dashboard exists but nobody understands what it's telling them
- A/B tests run for months because no one has the bandwidth to analyze results

## Solution

An AI Data Analyst that turns your raw data into clear insights and actionable recommendations — no SQL skills required.

## Key Features

### Automated Reports
Generates daily, weekly, and monthly performance reports and delivers them before your morning standup.

### Anomaly Detection
Alerts you the moment a key metric moves unexpectedly, with a hypothesis about what caused it.

### A/B Test Analysis
Analyzes experiment results with statistical rigor and tells you exactly what to do next.

### Forecasting
Projects revenue, churn, and growth metrics so you can plan with confidence instead of guesswork.

## Use Cases

- Analyze 12 months of user behavior data and surface the top three drop-off points
- Build a live revenue dashboard segmented by channel, plan, and cohort
- Run a full A/B test analysis and write a recommendation memo in one session
- Forecast next quarter's MRR based on current growth and churn trends
