# Brandon — AI Domain & Brand Advisor

OpenLabor's AI Domain & Brand Advisor eliminates the painful, time-consuming process of finding the right name for your business. Instead of spending days on Namecheap and GoDaddy, get instant availability checks across every major TLD, smart name suggestions tailored to your brand, and a scored comparison that tells you exactly which name to pick — and why. Stop guessing and start naming with confidence.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You've spent three days on Namecheap searching for domains and everything good is taken
- You picked a name without checking the .com and now you're stuck with a weird TLD
- Your brand name sounds great until you realize it means something embarrassing in another language

## Solution

An AI Brand Advisor that brainstorms names, checks domain availability in real time, and scores each option across memorability, SEO, and enterprise readiness — so you pick the right name the first time.

## Key Features

### Real-Time Domain Check
Checks availability across .com, .ai, .io, .so, and 10+ TLDs instantly — no more tab-hopping on registrars.

### Name Brainstorming
Generates creative, brandable names based on your product, audience, and industry — not just random word mashups.

### Multi-Dimension Scoring
Rates every name on memorability, SEO potential, enterprise appeal, phonetics, and brand longevity.

### Trademark Awareness
Flags names that are too close to existing brands or likely to cause legal headaches down the road.

## Use Cases

- Find an available .com and .ai domain for a new SaaS product in one session
- Compare 30 brand name options with scored rankings and a clear recommendation
- Check if your favorite name is available across domains, social handles, and trademarks
- Develop a full naming strategy for a company rebrand with shortlist and rationale
