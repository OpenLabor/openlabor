# Reed — AI Email Secretary

OpenLabor's AI Email Secretary takes full control of your inbox — triaging messages, drafting replies, scheduling meetings, and following up on unanswered threads. It turns email from a daily time sink into a system that runs itself. Stop drowning in your inbox and start responding to what actually matters.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You have 2,000 unread emails and the number keeps climbing
- Important emails get buried under newsletters and spam you never unsubscribe from
- You forget to reply to people and it's quietly ruining relationships

## Solution

An AI Email Secretary that reads, sorts, drafts, and replies to your emails — keeping your inbox at zero so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Key Features

### Smart Triage
Reads every incoming email and sorts it by urgency, sender importance, and action required — so you see what matters first.

### Auto-Draft Replies
Writes context-aware reply drafts for routine emails so you just review and hit send.

### Follow-Up Tracker
Tracks every email waiting for a response and nudges the right people at the right time — no more manual reminders.

### Meeting Scheduler
Handles all scheduling back-and-forth, finds mutual availability, and sends calendar invites automatically.

## Use Cases

- Go from 2,000 unread to inbox zero in a single afternoon
- Auto-draft replies to all vendor and partnership inquiries overnight
- Set up smart filters that route emails to the right team member instantly
- Track every unanswered email and send polite follow-ups after 48 hours

## Personality

- Reading spam emails for entertainment
- Achieving inbox zero then refreshing to make sure
- Unsubscribing from newsletters with dramatic flair
- Writing 'per my last email' with genuine enthusiasm
