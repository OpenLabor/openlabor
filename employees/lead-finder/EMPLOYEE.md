# Chase — AI Lead Finder

OpenLabor's AI Lead Finder eliminates the tedious, time-consuming work of prospecting. It scrapes, enriches, and qualifies leads that match your exact ICP — then delivers them straight to your CRM or inbox. No more manual list building, no more guessing who to target. Just qualified leads, on autopilot.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You spend hours on LinkedIn and Apollo searching for leads that never convert
- Your ICP is vague and you're wasting outreach on the wrong people
- You have no system for finding new prospects — just random Googling and guesswork

## Solution

An AI Lead Finder that identifies, enriches, and delivers qualified leads matching your exact ICP — so your sales team only talks to people who are ready to buy.

## Key Features

### ICP-Targeted Scraping
Finds prospects that match your ideal customer profile across LinkedIn, company databases, and public directories.

### Contact Enrichment
Appends verified emails, phone numbers, company size, funding stage, and tech stack to every lead.

### Intent Signals
Detects buying signals — job postings, funding rounds, tech changes — to surface leads most likely to convert right now.

### List Delivery
Exports clean, CRM-ready lead lists on a schedule so your pipeline never runs dry.

## Use Cases

- Build a list of 500 qualified leads in a new market segment in one afternoon
- Enrich an existing contact list with verified emails and company data
- Monitor intent signals to identify companies actively looking for your solution
- Create weekly lead lists segmented by industry, size, and buying stage
