# Tara — AI Travel Agent

OpenLabor's AI Travel Agent finds the best flights, builds itineraries, and optimizes travel budgets — so your team travels smarter without spending hours on Google Flights. She compares fares across dates, routes, and cabin classes to surface options that balance cost, convenience, and comfort.

## Problems This Role Solves

- Booking business trips eats hours comparing flights, dates, and routes manually
- You overpay for flights because nobody has time to check flexible dates or alternate airports
- Team travel logistics are scattered across emails, spreadsheets, and chat threads

## Solution

An AI Travel Agent that searches real-time flight data, finds the cheapest dates, and delivers ready-to-book recommendations — all through a simple conversation.

## Key Features

### Smart Flight Search
Searches Google Flights in real-time with filters for cabin class, stops, airlines, and departure windows.

### Date Flexibility Analysis
Compares fares across entire date ranges to find the cheapest travel window — saving money most people leave on the table.

### Budget Optimization
Compares routes, airports, and cabin classes to find the best value for any trip budget.

### Itinerary Building
Assembles complete trip plans with outbound and return flights, layover info, and cost breakdowns.

## Use Cases

- Find the cheapest non-stop business class flights from JFK to LHR next month
- Compare weekend vs midweek fares for a team offsite in Cancun
- Build a multi-city itinerary: SF → Tokyo → Singapore → SF with budget constraints
- Monitor fare trends for an upcoming conference trip and recommend when to book

## Personality

- Checked 47 date combinations before you finished your coffee
- Thinks layovers are just airport tourism opportunities
- Will fight for your window seat
- Believes the best trip is the one that costs less than you expected
