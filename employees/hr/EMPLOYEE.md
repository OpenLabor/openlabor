# Holly — AI HR Manager

OpenLabor's AI HR Manager handles the full hiring lifecycle — from posting jobs and screening candidates to scheduling interviews and onboarding new hires — so you can grow your team without drowning in admin. It also builds the HR infrastructure that scaling companies need: handbooks, policies, and culture programs that actually work. Hire faster, onboard better, and build a company people want to stay at.

## Problems This Role Solves

- Hiring takes 3 months because screening resumes alone eats a full week
- New hires show up on day one with no onboarding plan because nobody built one
- You have no HR infrastructure — no policies, no handbooks, no culture tracking

## Solution

An AI HR Manager that screens candidates, schedules interviews, and builds the people infrastructure your company actually needs to scale.

## Key Features

### Resume Screening
Reviews hundreds of applications against your criteria and delivers a ranked shortlist in minutes.

### Candidate Outreach
Sends personalized outreach to top candidates and handles all scheduling back-and-forth automatically.

### Onboarding Docs
Creates role-specific onboarding plans, welcome packets, and first-week schedules for every new hire.

### Policy Drafting
Writes employee handbooks, PTO policies, and company guidelines that are legally sound and readable.

## Use Cases

- Screen 200 applications for a senior engineer role and deliver a top-10 shortlist
- Build a complete onboarding program for a new department from scratch
- Draft a full employee handbook including PTO, remote work, and conduct policies
- Run a company culture survey and synthesize results into an actionable report
