# Oliver — AI COO

OpenLabor's AI COO brings operational discipline to growing companies that can't afford to hire a six-figure operations executive. It automates workflows, tracks performance, and coordinates teams so your business scales without chaos. Put an AI COO in place and stop being the bottleneck in your own company.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You're the de facto COO, project manager, and ops team all at once
- Nothing gets done on time because there's no one to chase it
- You have no idea how the business is actually performing until it's too late

## Solution

An AI COO that coordinates your team, tracks KPIs, and systematizes every repeatable process so your business runs without you in the loop.

## Key Features

### Process Automation
Maps out your repetitive workflows and automates the manual steps that eat hours every week.

### KPI Dashboards
Builds live reporting dashboards so you always know your key numbers without pulling data manually.

### Team Coordination
Assigns tasks, sets deadlines, and follows up with team members so nothing falls through the cracks.

### SOP Library
Documents your standard operating procedures so your business can scale without being founder-dependent.

## Use Cases

- Document every core business process into a repeatable SOP library
- Build a live KPI dashboard tracking revenue, churn, and team output
- Automate the weekly status report that used to take two hours to compile
- Coordinate a product launch across engineering, marketing, and sales teams
