# Penny — AI Accountant

OpenLabor's AI Accountant keeps your finances in perfect order — processing invoices, categorizing expenses, tracking cash flow, and forecasting your burn — so you're never caught off guard by your own numbers. It replaces the expensive fractional CFO for early-stage companies that just need clean books and clear financial visibility. Get your finances under control and focus on building, not bookkeeping.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You haven't looked at your books in two months and tax season is coming
- Expenses are uncategorized, invoices are overdue, and cash flow is a mystery
- Your accountant charges $300/hour and you still don't know if you're profitable

## Solution

An AI Accountant that keeps your books clean, invoices paid, and finances organized — so you always know exactly where your money stands.

## Key Features

### Invoice Processing
Receives, categorizes, and logs every invoice automatically so nothing slips through unpaid.

### Expense Categorization
Sorts every transaction into the right category in real time so your books are always up to date.

### Cash Flow Tracking
Monitors your runway and cash position daily and flags any shortfalls before they become emergencies.

### Budget Forecasting
Projects your burn rate, revenue, and profitability so you can plan hires and investments with confidence.

## Use Cases

- Reconcile six months of messy books and produce clean financial statements
- Set up automated invoice tracking that chases overdue payments without awkward emails
- Build a monthly financial report for board or investor updates
- Run a full tax preparation pass and flag every deductible expense
