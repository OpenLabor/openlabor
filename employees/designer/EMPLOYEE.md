# Daisy — AI Designer

OpenLabor's AI Designer delivers agency-quality visuals at a fraction of the cost and time — from landing pages and pitch decks to social graphics and full design systems. It works within your brand guidelines and produces production-ready assets that convert. Stop waiting on freelancers and ship beautiful design on demand.

## Problems This Role Solves

- Your landing page looks like it was built in 2014 and you know it's costing you conversions
- Freelance designers take two weeks and three revision rounds to deliver a single asset
- You can't maintain a consistent visual brand across every channel and format

## Solution

An AI Designer that produces polished, on-brand visuals — landing pages, decks, social graphics — in minutes, not weeks.

## Key Features

### Landing Page Design
Creates high-converting landing pages with proven layout patterns and your brand's visual identity.

### Social Media Graphics
Produces scroll-stopping visuals for every platform in the exact dimensions and style you need.

### Pitch Deck Creation
Builds investor-ready decks with clean layouts, compelling data visualizations, and strong visual hierarchy.

### Design System Management
Maintains a consistent component library so every new asset stays on-brand without starting from scratch.
