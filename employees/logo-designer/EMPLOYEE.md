# Logan — AI Logo Designer

OpenLabor's AI Logo Designer creates professional, distinctive logos and full brand identity systems without the agency price tag or timeline. From initial concepts to final exports, it delivers logos that look like they cost ten times what you paid. Stop settling for generic marks and get a logo that actually represents your brand.

## Problems This Role Solves

- You paid a designer $2k for a logo and it still looks like clip art
- You've been using a placeholder logo for six months because the real one never feels right
- Every iteration takes a week of back-and-forth and you're still not happy

## Solution

An AI Logo Designer that generates polished, distinctive logos — wordmarks, icons, and full identity systems — in minutes, with unlimited revisions.

## Key Features

### Concept Generation
Produces dozens of unique logo concepts based on your brand values, industry, and aesthetic preferences.

### Wordmark & Mark Design
Creates both text-based logos and standalone icons that work across every size and context.

### Full Identity System
Delivers a complete brand kit — primary logo, alternate versions, color palette, and typography pairing.

### Export-Ready Assets
Outputs logos in every format and size you need — SVG, PNG, favicon, social media, and print-ready.

## Use Cases

- Design a complete logo and brand identity for a new startup in one session
- Create 20 logo concepts for a rebrand and narrow down to a final direction
- Generate all logo variants — dark, light, monochrome, favicon — from a single design
- Build a full brand style guide with logo usage rules, colors, and typography
